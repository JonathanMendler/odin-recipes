Roasted chicken

A Picture of culinary bliss!

Picture this: a golden-brown bird, perfectly roasted to juicy, succulent perfection, its skin crackling with savory goodness. The aroma alone is enough to make your mouth water - a tantalizing blend of herbs, spices, and roasting juices that fills the air with anticipation.

Ingredients

  • Whole Chicken
  • Olive Oil
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Pat chicken dry and truss the legs.
  2. Rub chicken with oil and pat in the seasoning.
  3. Roast chicken in the oven at 425°F for 70 - 90 minutes, until beutifully golden.
  4. Feast!
