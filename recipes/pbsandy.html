Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandy

The simple yet glorious delight of a peanut butter and jelly sandwich!

Picture this: two slices of soft, pillowy bread embracing a generous spread of creamy peanut butter, each dollop a heavenly indulgence. But wait, it gets even better - a layer of sweet, velvety jelly, bursting with fruity goodness, creating a symphony of flavors that dance harmoniously on your palate.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

Steps

  1. Smear peanut butter on 1 slice of bread.
  2. Smear jelly on other slice of bread. The ratio of jelly to peanut butter should be about 2:1
  3. Push bread together, with peanut butter and jelly embacing each other.
  4. Consume!
