Barbacoa

Oh, let me tell you about Barbacoa!

Picture this: tender, succulent meat, slow-cooked to absolute perfection, infused with a symphony of spices and flavors that will make your taste buds dance with joy. Barbacoa is not just any ordinary dish; it's a culinary masterpiece, a celebration of Mexican cuisine at its finest.

Ingredients

  • Chuck Roast
  • Beef Broth
  • Chipotle Chilis
  • Spices
  • Fresh Lime Juice

Steps

  1. Cut roast into 6 portions while removing any large pieces of fat.
  2. Heat skillet and sear seasoned beef, half at a time, until browned on all sides.
  3. In a food processor, pulse together chipotle chilies, spices, and beef broth until well pureed.
  4. Pour mixture over beef in slow cooker.
  5. Cover and cook on low heat 8 - 9 hours.
  6. Remove and shred beef
  7. Stir lime juice into broth in slow cooker then return beef to slow cooker and cook on low or warm 20 - 30 minutes longer.
  8. Devour!
